The Perfect Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is awesome. Everyone loves it, including children. The noodles are fun to eat because you get to slurp them up like you are a human vaccuum cleaner.

People that don't like spaghetti are probably not very smart. If you know someone that doesn't like spaghetti, you may want to consider going no-contact with them immediately.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of dry spaghetti noodles
  • 1 jar of your favorite spaghetti sauce
  • 1 package of frozen Italian-style meatballs
  • Shredded parmesan cheese (as much as you'd like, don't hold back)

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles until they are soft but not too soft.
  2. Feed one of the noodles to your dog and see if she approves
  3. Strain the noodles and return to pot
  4. Add some noodles to a plate and then add some sauce from the pan. You should have been heating this up by the way, oops.
  5. Add meatballs on top. You were supposed to heat those up too, according to packaging instructions (duh)
  6. Add a bunch of parmesan cheese to the top.
  7. Enjoy!
